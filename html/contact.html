How it Works

Movius is a leading provider of converged messaging and call completion solutions for telecommunication providers around the world.

Its real-time applications for converged messaging and mobile identities can be used on any device and across any network. Movius’ applications for converged messaging, and mobile identities level the playing field for mobile operators by allowing them to offer services using the same business models offered by OTT providers. In doing so, it helps them to increase ARPU and widen their market share.

Over 150 of the world’s leading MVNOs, enterprises and service providers leverage Movius’ mobile applications to address the security, privacy and billing issues associated with corporate BYOD/CYOD programs. Movius’ solutions are also currently integrated with partner solutions, including Samsung KNOX, OpenPeak ADAM, and Cellrox Multi Persona.
